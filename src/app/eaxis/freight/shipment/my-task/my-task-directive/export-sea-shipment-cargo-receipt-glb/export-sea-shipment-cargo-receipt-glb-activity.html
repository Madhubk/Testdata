• For factory stuffing containers we capture the date of container Gate In at Port CY or ICD (for rail linked ICD movements). Note that for ICD movements we are capturing receipt date at ICD for rail out. We are not capturing arrival of train into CY. • For dock stuffing cargo we capture the date of carting of cargo into CFS/ICD. Carting refers to the arrival of cargo into a CFS/ICD and off-loading into our designated carting shed. Note that dock stuffing shipments will also have a separate transport leg from CFS/ICD up to CY. We are not capturing this movement.
