Product Bulk Upload

Instructions
ProductInsert

1. Product Insert option is used to simply Validate the Product Details and Insert those details.

Product Insert & Update

1. Product Insert & Update option is used to insert new Product Details as well as update the existing product details.
2. In Product General Details except Product Code all the details can be updated.
3. In RelatedParty, it allows to update existing related organization details and also Insert new Organization.
4. In Unit conversion, it allows only to insert new conversion details.
5. In Barcode, it allows only to insert new barcode details.
